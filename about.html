About Helping Hands

Learn about our journey, mission, and the team behind our community work

Our Story

Helping Hands was founded in 2015 by a group of dedicated volunteers in Johannesburg who saw the growing need to combat hunger and homelessness in our communities.

What started as a small initiative with a few volunteers has grown into a registered non-profit organization that serves hundreds of families each month.

Over the years, we have expanded our services to include food parcels, clothing donations, temporary shelter, and educational programs, always guided by our core values of compassion and service.

Our Journey Image

Our Mission

To uplift underprivileged communities through compassion, service, and sustainable programs that address immediate needs while working towards long-term solutions.

Our Vision

A future where every person in Johannesburg has access to basic necessities, dignity, and the opportunity to build a better life for themselves and their families.
