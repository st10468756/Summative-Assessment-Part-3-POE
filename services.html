Our Services

Comprehensive support programs designed to uplift underprivileged communities

Food Parcels Image

Food Parcel Distribution

We provide nutritious food parcels to families and individuals facing food insecurity. Each parcel contains essential items to sustain a family for up to two weeks.

  • Monthly distribution to registered families
  • Emergency food parcels for crisis situations
  • Special dietary requirements accommodated
  • Nutritional education and cooking workshops
500+ Families served monthly
10,000+ Meals provided monthly

Clothing Donations

We collect and distribute quality clothing to keep people warm, dignified, and prepared for job interviews and daily life.

  • Seasonal clothing distributions
  • Professional attire for job seekers
  • School uniforms for children
  • Baby clothing and essentials
2,000+ People clothed monthly
50+ Corporate clothing drives
Clothing Donations Image
Shelter Image

Temporary Shelter

We provide safe, temporary accommodation for homeless individuals and families while working towards permanent housing solutions.

  • Safe overnight accommodation
  • Family units for parents with children
  • Basic hygiene facilities
  • Case management and support services
100+ Beds available
75% Successful transitions to permanent housing

Education & Skills Development

We offer educational support and skills development programs to help individuals gain independence and improve their employment prospects.

  • Adult literacy classes
  • Computer skills training
  • Job readiness workshops
  • After-school tutoring for children
  • Vocational training programs
300+ Students enrolled annually
65% Employment rate after completion
